## Definition
An ERD is a 
- Visual representation
- Illustrates the relationships and interactions between entities in a database. 
- Showcases the logical structure of a database system displaying entities and the relationships between them as lines connecting these boxes
- Box :  Entity
- Line : Relationship
## Component 
**Entity:** 
- Represent people, object, concept, et cetera,...
- Serve as fundamental building block
- Form the basic for relationship
**Attributes:**
- Denote characteristic or properties of an entity
- Depict as oval 
**Relationship set: **
- Illustrate how entities are interconnected
- Show the relation between instances
- Represent connections between different entities
**Crow's foot notations: **
- Indicates relationship between entities
- Employs symbols to signify nature and quantity
- Provides a clear depiction of the relationship
