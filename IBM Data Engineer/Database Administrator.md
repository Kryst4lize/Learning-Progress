
A day of data administrator: 
- **Morning Routine**: The DBA, Rygel, starts his day by checking the status of the databases he manages using preconfigured dashboards. He resolves a minor alert without issues.
    
- **Support Tickets**: Rygel reviews user requests that typically come in as support tickets. He addresses slow-running queries and collaborates with a developer on a feature improvement for a marketing campaign database.
    
- **Planning Meeting**: He attends a meeting with developers and data engineers to discuss a new database design, focusing on performance and resource allocation for future growth.
    
- **Analytics and Configuration**: Rygel uses analytics to determine server resources and configures alerts to monitor database activity.
