- **Definition of a Staging Area**: A staging area is an intermediate storage space used in the [[ETL]] (Extract, Transform, Load) process. It serves as a bridge between data sources and target [[Data Warehouse]], [[Datamart]], [[Datalake]], or other repositories.
    
- **Purpose of Staging Areas**:
    
    - **Integration**: Consolidates data from multiple source systems.
    - **Change Detection**: Manages the extraction of new and modified data.
    - **Scheduling**: Allows for the scheduling of ETL tasks in a specific sequence.
    - **Data Cleansing and Validation**: Handles missing values and duplicates.
    - **Aggregation**: Summarizes data for reporting purposes.
    - **Normalization**: Ensures consistency in data types and naming conventions.
